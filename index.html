Super Bowl 4th and Goal

It's 4th and Goal with 1 second left in the SuperBowl! The Browns are down to Packers 35 to 40. They hand the ball to stud running back Nick Chubb. If you can cross the opponents goaline then the Browns win, if you go out of bounds they lose. You can control the ball carrier with the arrow keys. *hint* You can run through all other players on the field because in this situation no1 would ever be able to tackle Nick Chubb! *Note* This game is designed for a full screen browser.
